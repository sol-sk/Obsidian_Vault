SS is the total sum of squared deviations
- Dividing this by N gives us mean squared deviation = **variance**
### SS (sum of squares) = $\sum{X^2 - \frac{(\sum{X})^2}{N}}$

### s (standard deviation, alt.) = $\sqrt{\frac{SS}{N-1}}$
